## ðŸ§® Tokenomics Math (Minting Curve)

For memes with bonding curve:

- `P(n) = a + b * n`
  - `P`: price of mint `n`
  - `a`: base price (e.g. 0.0001 ETH)
  - `b`: slope (e.g. 0.00002 ETH)

Result: early buyers get cheaper mints, price increases predictably with demand.
